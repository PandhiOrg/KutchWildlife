Annual Contest

Photography
Competition 2020

Submit your entry

Rules

  • You must be a resident of Kutch.
  • You can submit up to 5 photos.
  • You must be at least 18 years old.
  • Must be your own photo.
  • Must not be a winner or runner up photo in other previous contests.
  • Must be a photo of a species of Kutch, whether a native, migratory, or a vagrant. It must be either a bird, reptile, or mammal.
  • Color correction and minor enhancements allowed, but photo manipulation is strictly forbidden.
  • Must not publish the photo anywhere else until after the contest ends.
  • Photo must not be older than one year.

Submission Guidelines

  • Image must be at least 2000 pixels on either dimension.
  • For the submission and judging process, watermarks are not allowed. But your watermark will be added for the final published version. Entries with watermark may be returned or disqualified.
  • Ensure the submission file is named in this format Your_Full_Name_SubmissionNumber.jpg. For example, Raju_Shah_5.jpg.
  • Image must be in JPEG format, at 90% quality or higher (10% compression or less).
  • EXIF data should be intact on the image (f/stop, ISO, shutter speed, etc.). You may remove location/GPS data if any.

Deadline

  • Entries must be submitted by December 31, 2020 (11:59PM IST).
  • Winners will be announced on January 9, 2021.

Judging Process

  • Each image will be given a rating between 1 to 10 in the categories of aesthetics + composition, animal behavior, and overall ????.
  • Winners will be decided from the total score.
  • In the case of the same person being in the top 3, they will be recognized for the higher scored image only. The subsequent positions will be given to the next highest scoring Submission.

Prizes

  • 1st Rs. 5000
  • 2nd Rs. 2500
  • 3rd Rs. 1000
  • All 3 will be featured on the species page, permanent contest history page, etc.
  • Top 20 submissions will receive a special certificate for honorable mentions.
  • All participants will receive a digital certificate.
  • Every participant has a chance to get their photos featured on the species page. Best of the best can get features as the primary image for that species.

Judges

  • Mukul Soman
  • Kimberly Tripp
  • Nikhil Whitaker
