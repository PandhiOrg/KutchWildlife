Credits and Acknowledgments

This website is created and maintained by Mahim Pandhi Wildlife Foundation with the help of students, researchers, and other volunteers from the public at large.

Every effort has been made to ensure accuracy of the data. However, because of the large quantity of data, and automated solutions for curating it may incur some minor errors. If you find any inaccuracies, please write us.

We are grateful to the Forest Department of Kutch (West Division) for their support, with thanks to Dr. Tushar Patel.

Special thanks to our friends at SerpApi for their invaluable assistence in collecting research data.

Unless otherwise stated, the main content for the species pages is provided by Wikipedia. Additional metrics cited from Animal Diversity Web.
