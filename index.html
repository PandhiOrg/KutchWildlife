KutchWildlife.com

The importance
of our wildlife

Kutch has an abundance of wildlife, and an abundance of opportunities for research. However, there is no central repository for all information regarding the native wildlife species, or research conducted upon them.

This non-profit website is provided as a public service, where all the data is contributed by the people, and owned by the people. Our goal is foster open exchange between students, researchers, naturalists, and citizens so we can better learn about our natural heritage, and protect it while we still have a chance.

About The Initiative

Fostering open exchange

In the spirit of fostering open exchange between researchers, and even citizen scientists, the Kutch Wildlife Initiative was created to become a central hub for everything related to wildlife in Kutch.

As a pure Open Source initiative, this databank is meant to belong to the public. As the data grows, researchers both local and from around the world, will have access to vital information about Kutch's wildlife. Our goal is to help usher in a new era especially for a new generation of researchers.

Get involved

Contribute
to the databank

This databank is created by the public, for the public. Whether you are a professional researcher or a citizen scientist, you are welcome to contribute to this website and help it grow. You can contribute new content, suggest edits, report inaccuracies, and more.
