FOR THE PEOPLE
BY THE PEOPLE

KutchWildlife.com is built to be an ever-evolving resource for both researchers and the public. As a public service initiative, the website and the database will grow over several phases.

Data, photos, and other information is provided by volunteers. If you would like to help grow this resource, please get in touch. You do not need to be a professional scientist - just havin a passion for wildlife is all you need.
